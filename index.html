Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a dynamic field at the intersection of computer science and engineering principles, focusing on the development and application of computer systems and software. It encompasses the study of algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and computer architecture. CSE professionals design, develop, and analyze various technologies, including software applications, computer networks, and operating systems. The field also delves into emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cybersecurity. With its diverse applications across industries, CSE plays a pivotal role in shaping the technological landscape and driving innovation in the digital era.
